I am a PhD candidate in economics at the RITM (Paris-Saclay) under the supervision of Matthieu Crozet. My research interests include Migration, Spatial Economics, and Economic History. I am currently focusing on the drivers of contemporary and historical internal migration using empirical and structural methods.

Here you can find information about my research projects, teaching materials, and professional activities. Feel free to contact me at alex.newnham[at]universite-paris-saclay.fr.
