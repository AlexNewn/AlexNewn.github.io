I am a PhD candidate in economics at the RITM (Paris-Saclay) under the supervision of Matthieu Crozet.

I am primarily interested in Migration, Spatial Economics and Economic History. My current work aims to understand the drivers of contemporary and historical internal migration using a combination of empirical and structural methods.

I study what enables or disables individuals and households to move, focusing on understanding the recent decrease in mobility that many countries have experienced.
